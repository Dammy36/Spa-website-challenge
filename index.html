Every Customer Counts

The Damsel Nails & Spa opened for business on June 6th 2016 in the converted kitchen of a small shopping complex. The Nail Studio was one of the first nail salons at the time in community. Since then we are proud to have grown into a multi-purpose beauty centre offering a long list of services.

The Damsel Nails & Spa is located on a quiet street in the financial hub of Lagos. With over 40 members of staff, the Nail Studio offers a wide range of services to everyone.
